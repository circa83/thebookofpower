Philosophy

The Book of Power is a framework for developing and sustaining expanded states — a way to carry the clarity of meditation, ceremony, or breathwork into daily life.

The framework centers on mastering attention and strengthening intent, expressed through two core methods: Element Meditation and Alpha Dreaming.

Resonance Rituals

The framework comes alive through immersive ambient compositions. Longform soundscapes are intentionally created to frame intention, hold space for practice, and deepen integration. Whether guiding a group or sitting alone, the music provides the container: steady, grounding, and alive.

Compositions weave modular synthesis, ambient guitar, and field recordings into somatic listening experiences designed to guide awareness inward and deepen ritual.
